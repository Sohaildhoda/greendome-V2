Who We Are

Green Dome Capital is a privately-owned financial institution in South Africa that assists clients to access business funding through commercial banks, Development Finance Institutions and Government programs.

We look at our clients’ business holistically, assess their business and financial needs and offer a best-in-business advisory support to grow the business to the next level

Our Services

Green Dome Capital unlocks your company’s potential while you focus on what is important – YOUR BUSINESS. We assist small and medium enterprise (SME) by providing them with guidance and assistance to access funds to 

  • fund new projects,
  • expand their business or refurbishments
  • existing manufacturers who wish to expand or modernise their production capacity

We identify businesses who qualify for funding from Development Finance institutions (Eg IDC, DBSA) and the DTIC. This can be in structured in several ways: -

  • Debt
  • Equity and quasi-equity
  • Guarantees
  • Trade Finance
  • Venture Capital
  • BBBEE Management services
  • SMME Development

Moreover, Green Dome Capital offers tailormade non-financial business support for companies. This includes 

  • Developing a solid business strategy, business plan and financial modelling
  • Assisting clients to maintain/move up their B-BEE level in terms of Skills Development learnerships for unemployed youth through the ETI system
  • Provide software solutions to manage procurement and BEE Scorecard management
  • Create collaborations and market access
  • The Black Industrialist Scheme (BIS) under the Departments of Trade and Industry’s (DTI)
  • Green Energy Funding
